About Maria

My Journey

I know exactly how it feels to be in your shoes. I used to be unhappy with how I looked and shopping for clothes that fit used to be a nightmare. I had health issues such as high blood pressure and suffered through thyroid issues. I also developed depression and anxiety and used to drink heavily to feel better.


After having my second child Sophia, I was 60lbs heavier than I am now, and suffered through postpartum depression. I was in a terrible place in my life and had 0 self confidence. I knew I needed a change for my kids, myself and for my health.



That's when a friend suggested I should start working out. I was totally clueless on how to start, but I was commited. I started with a workout program and lost 20lbs in 12 weeks and I felt Amazing! I fell in love with the results I saw and I couldn't stop! Even on the days where I felt unmotivated, I pushed my excuses to the side and made it happen.



It was more than just losing weight, I gained so much more from that experience! I started to feel more confident, love myself and now i'm happier than I've ever been! Without investing in myself, I would never have been able to make that change in my life. Now I want to share my story with others and motivate them to create a better version of themselves because I know YOU can do it. I believe in you.
