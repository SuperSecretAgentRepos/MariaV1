Who I Am

My name is Maria Perez, a full time fitness expert. I am a Certified personal trainer, go-getter and mother of 2. Originated in NYC I started off just like all of my clients. I needed to make a change for my family and myself due too health issues. I was obese weighing 225 lbs with high blood pressure, emotional eating habits, suffering from anxiety and depression. I had to take the first steps to making a change, I pushed my excuses to the side and made it happen. I started off with a workout program. I felt the need to share my jouney with others because I beleive if I could do it so can you. The key to success is believing in yourself. I want to help others succeed just like myself.
